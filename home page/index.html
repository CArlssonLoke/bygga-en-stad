(GPU) The GPU or the Graphics Processing Unit, is the main purpose of the GPU is to accelerate graphics rendering. This means that without any form of a GPU the computer will not be able to display anything. Some CPU's have integrated GPU's but most of them don't. The GPU is like a big strong muscle, which is very good at doing a few things but not very good at doing a lot of different things. It renders graphics in games and does advanced calculations very very well. The GPU is almost always the most expensive part of the PC, especially if it's a gaming PC. The two largest manufacturers of GPU's is AMD or Nvidia. Nvidia with its RTX and GTX line and AMD with their Radeon RX line of graphics card. The GPU also has a tiny bit of very very fast RAM which is used to keep textures in mind when they are currently not being rendered and for other similar things.
